Our Laboratory

One of the Region's Best-Equipped Labs

The laboratory is a key department in disease diagnosis and management. Ours is among the best-equipped in the region, led by a senior laboratory technologist with more than ten years of experience.

We take part in External Quality Assurance schemes and partner with Lancet Kenya and Pathcare Kenya, so results you receive here meet trusted national standards.

  • Senior technologist with 10+ years' experience
  • External Quality Assurance participation
  • Partnerships with Lancet Kenya & Pathcare Kenya
Departments

Our Clinical Departments

Haematology

Examination of blood samples to detect anaemia, leukaemia and clotting disorders.

Clinical Chemistry

Analysis of blood and body fluids to assess organ function and metabolic disorders.

Microbiology

Identification of bacteria, viruses and fungi behind infections.

Immunology

Evaluation of the immune system using ELISA and immunofluorescence.

Molecular Diagnostics

Detection of genetic markers to guide personalized treatment approaches.
