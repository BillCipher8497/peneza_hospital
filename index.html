Who We Are

About Peneza Hospital

Peneza Healthcare Ltd, trading as Peneza Hospital, is a duly registered Level 4 private hospital, licensed by the Kenya Medical Practitioners and Dentists Council (KMPDC) to operate within Rongo Sub-County, Migori County, Kenya.

The hospital has been operational since 8th January 2024, providing quality healthcare services to residents of Rongo Sub-County, Migori County, and the neighboring counties within the Nyanza region.

Meet Our Team →
Our Vision

The hospital's vision is to provide Patient-Centered Quality health Care while upholding the highest standards of medical ethics, alleviating suffering, restoring health, and saving lives.

Through its commitment to excellence, professionalism, ethical medical practice, and compassionate care, Peneza Hospital continues to play a vital role in improving access to quality healthcare services within Migori County and the wider Nyanza region.
