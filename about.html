About Peneza Hospital

Home › About

At Peneza Hospital, we believe in a team-based approach to healthcare.

Who We Are

Peneza Hospital is a private hospital based in Rongo, Kenya, built to bring dependable, modern healthcare close to home for our community.

Our Approach

Doctors, nurses, technologists and support staff work as one team around each patient, sharing information so care is coordinated and complete.

Our Commitment

We are committed to dignity, safety and quality, treating every patient the way we would want our own family treated.

Our Team

From the reception desk to the operating theatre, our people are experienced, compassionate and present 24 hours a day.
